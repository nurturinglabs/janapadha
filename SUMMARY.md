## ğŸ¯ What is BAREYIRI?

**BAREYIRI** is a noble educational initiative that goes far beyond a typical competition. It's a movement to:
- **Promote Writing Skills** - Essential for every child
- **Encourage Reading** - The foundation of great writing
- **Celebrate Bilingualism** - Equal honor to English and Kannada
- **Preserve Handwriting** - The art of pen and paper
- **Build Community** - Bangalore localities united

---

## âœ¨ Unique Aspects

### 1. **Handwritten, In-Person Competition**
Unlike digital competitions, BAREYIRI requires students to:
- Write by hand on paper
- Attend competition venue in person
- Submit physical papers
- Demonstrate penmanship skills

**Why?**
- Develops cognitive engagement
- Preserves traditional craft
- Ensures authenticity
- Builds community experience

### 2. **Bilingual Platform**
- **English** - Global connectivity
- **Kannada** - Cultural preservation
- Website available in both languages
- Students choose language preference
- Equal respect for both

### 3. **Quarterly Model**
- 4 competitions per year
- Each quarter = new category
- Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4 schedule
- Regular practice opportunities
- Different Bangalore localities each quarter
